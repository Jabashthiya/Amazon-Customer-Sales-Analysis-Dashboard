# Amazon-Customer-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Amazon sales data and generate business insights related to revenue, customers, products, brands, and geographic performance.

**Tools Used**
> Power BI
> DAX
> Microsoft Excel

**Key KPIs**
Total Customers: 100K
Total Revenue: 91.83M
Total Quantity Sold: 300K
Average Shipping Cost: $7.41
Average Tax Amount
Repeat Customers: 30K

**Key Insights**
Generated total revenue of 91.83M from 100K customers.
Approximately 30% of customers are repeat customers.
Electronics emerged as one of the top-performing categories.
UrbanStyle recorded the highest average sales among brands.
Sales remained relatively stable throughout the year.
The United States contributed the largest share of revenue.
Top customers generated significant revenue through multiple purchases.
Average shipping cost was maintained at $7.41 per order.
